About Njorden

Njorden Automotive was born from a singular belief: that true luxury doesn’t shout—it whispers. Inspired by the Norse god Njord, guardian of wind, sea, and wealth, our brand embraces harmony with nature, movement with meaning, and design with depth.

Our Philosophy

At Njorden, we build more than cars—we craft experiences. Vehicles that glide through the Nordic landscape in silence, powered by hydrogen, shaped by heritage, and wrapped in sustainable elegance. Our commitment is to timeless design, responsibly made. Analogue, not artificial. Crafted, not manufactured.

Sander Skarre Lindseth

Founder & Visionary

“I created Njorden because I believe the future of luxury must be as clean as it is beautiful. We owe it to our planet—and ourselves—to drive differently.”
