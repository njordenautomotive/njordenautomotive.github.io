Our Journey

This isn’t just the story of a car company.

It’s the beginning of a movement—one that believes true luxury should be clean, quiet, and conscious. Njorden Automotive was born from the Nordic landscape and the myth of Njord, god of wind, sea, and wealth. Our mission is to craft vehicles that honor that spirit: effortless movement, natural harmony, and timeless elegance.

We’re building a new kind of car—one that speaks softly but carries deep meaning. Hydrogen-powered. Analogue in soul. Designed for those who want more than status—they want stillness.

Where We Are Now

We’re at the beginning. Our design language is forming. Materials are being sourced. Concepts are in motion. The first prototypes are ahead.
